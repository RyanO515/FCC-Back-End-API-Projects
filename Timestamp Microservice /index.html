Timestamp Microservice API

FreeCodeCamp Basejump




To format your date within the URL:

  • The timestamp must either be a Unix or natural date timestamp
  • Ex. Unix Timestamp:
    - 1450137600
  • Ex. Natural Timestamp:
    - April 4, 2016
